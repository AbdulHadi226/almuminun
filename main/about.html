Abous us.

We are an Islamic Discord server (Sunni Salafi) open to everyone, regardless of their religion, ethnicity, or race. Our platform is dedicated to spreading Islam (da'wah), fostering Brotherhood/Sisterhood among Muslims, and guiding non-Muslims towards Islam. Our aim is to unite Muslims, fostering a sense of togetherness and solidarity. Rather than remaining divided, we strive to come together as one, forming one united Ummah. Non-Muslims are welcome to join our Server. We're here to introduce you to Islam in a welcoming and supportive environment. Everyone is welcome to join us.
