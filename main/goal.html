What is our goal?

Our objective is to introduce Islam to those who are not yet Muslim. We aim to offer guidance to as many individuals as possible, inviting them to embrace Islam. Central to our mission is to promote a positive image of Islamâ€”one that is not associated with terrorism or extremism. We emphatically reject violence and terrorism. Like everyone else, we are ordinary human beings. Our approach is peaceful; we seek to spread Islam through kindness and understanding. We do not coerce or compel anyone to convert, as this contradicts the principles of our faith. Instead, we strive to foster connections and friendships among people. This server serves as a platform for respectful debates, sharing knowledge about Islam (da'wah), making friends, and expanding social circles. Our goal is to build Brotherhood / Sisterhood with Muslims.
